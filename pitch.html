About

I'm a 33-year-old poker pro, entrepreneur, and influencer from Illinois. I was born with a rare neuromuscular disorder called Spinal Muscular Atrophy (SMA) which can best be described as something close to being completely paralyzed from the neck down, but I can feel absolutely everything. Therefore, I need assistance with nearly all aspects of my day-to-day life. My parents are my primary caregivers, and I couldn't be luckier because they truly are 2 of my best friends, and we love being around each other. When I play live, both of them travel with me to take care of everything I need, and my dad is always the one sitting beside me, showing me my cards and moving my chips. Anyone who ever meets us never forgets it!
