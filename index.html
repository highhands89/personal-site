Welcome! K.L. Cleeton

Is an entrepreneur, poker player, and influencer who empowers individuals to realize their potential.

He focuses on startup development and motivates others to overcome their limitations and achieve more than they ever imagined.

Currently, he's an advisor to Vendoor, an application that enhances efficiency and streamlines operations in the construction and retail sectors.

A Short Intro to K.L.

K.L. was born with a rare neuromuscular disorder called Spinal Muscular Atrophy (SMA) which can best be described as something close to being completely paralyzed from the neck down, but he can feel absolutely everything.

While he needs assistance with nearly all aspects of is day-to-day life, with his parents as his primary caregivers, he loves and lives life to the fullest!
